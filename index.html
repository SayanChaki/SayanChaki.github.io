Artificial Minds
"I do not paint what I see — I paint what must be known."

Postdoctoral researcher at École Centrale de Lyon and Inria, working within the ANR DIKE project on fairness in Large Language Models and multi-agent resource allocation. My work stands at the intersection of formal mathematics and the living, breathing dynamics of intelligent systems.

My PhD research — completed at Inria, Laboratoire Hubert Curien, and Université Jean Monnet — pioneered advances in equivariant generative architectures, bringing group theory and PAC-Bayesian frameworks to bear on object decomposition and historical document analysis. I have delivered invited talks at Oxford, Vienna, and Wolfenbüttel.
